The Holy Lands Of Pizzora

Pizzora was the lands that the needy would seek, and the opportunists would be attracted to, a place of warmth, peace and fortune for those of cunning mind and quick wits. Pizzora lay on the west side of the continent, a great port on the aldoonian sea, its strategic location brought it great riches, with them the city grew and flourished.

Pizzora was a theocratic nation, ruled by the hands of the high priest of pizzora, and beneath him were his acolytes who uphold the law and justice. The citizens of pizzora cherished the high priests and his church, they gather twice a week for social and religious activities, and many times through the year in annual festivals.

The reign of peace and bless of pizzora, came to an abrupt halt, and everything changed when the Heretic armies attacked, sacking and setting the city ablaze. A once peaceful nation, swear a sacred vow to rid the world of the Heretic scum. Consumed with hatred, the once peaceful people of Pizzora transformed into dark, mean creatures, no more so, than the High priest himself.


The high Priest Of Pizzora

The high priest was the ruler of Pizzora, the hand of the deity that governed the affairs of the lands, chosen through a religious ceremony; the Priest would make the Great palace of Picaira his own.

Serving him were a group of twelve Acolytes, these acolytes upheld the law and attended the religious festivals. As the Priest was the hand, the Acolytes were the fingers that governed the land and were responsible for the growth and welfare of the nation.

All this changed after the Heretic armies attacked, the High priest turn dark with lust for the heretics’ blood, he turned to dark powers to achieve his goal of terminating the Heretics in his beloved lands, he acquired forgotten knowledge, knowledge that was best left forgotten.

The high priest learnt new forms of magical power that he used in the war against the heretics but slowly this power started to infect the Priest and his acolytes, twisting their beliefs and minds, turning them to paths of cruelty, and with this power the mighty city of Pizzora transformed from the paradise it was to a city full of horror, shadow and nightmare.


The Heretic Kurrath The Mad

Kurrath the mad, was born of a pristine family in the lands of Pizzora, he spent his youth in the streets of pizzora, in those days this was quite safe, he loved the city and its people, and would go around in his free time helping the folk out in their work.

At the age of ten, Kurrath suffered two tragedies, the first was the banishment of his family to the country and the ceasing of their wealth by the hands of the High priest, this brought a change in the life style of Kurrath, as there wasn’t anyone around to talk to, and he missed the city.

The second tragedy was the death of his father, where kurrath found his father hanging one night in the barn. Kurrath blamed the High priest for his father’s death, as the banishment by the priest led to his father taking his own life.

Kurrath swore that one day he will bring justice to his father, and rid the lands of the filth of the Pizzorian church that in his eyes mind was suppressing its people.


Captain Tad The Pirate

Before Tad joined the criminal life and became a pirate, he was a respected citizen of pizzora, he owned a inn on the western side of city, near the docks. His inn was respectable for an inn near the docks, business was good and he made lots of profit.

Alas, he witnessed firsthand the severe changes to the city after the Heretic attacks, Tad was greatly affected by the attacks; his inn had caught fire and by the time the fires were extinguished half the inn was gone, along with his wealth. As he couldn’t run a inn anymore, Tad turned the ruined inn into a grimy bar at the docks.

But Tad’s miseries didn’t end there, after getting into trouble some noble men he was forced to flee the city with a bunch of sailors, the captain of the ship agreed to take Tad in as a deck hands, quickly tad grew to know the traded as if he did all his life.

After years at sea, Tad had acquired ships and crews, and was able to assemble an armada of pirate ships, and he set sail back to the lands of pizzora, seeking vengeance on those whom wronged him.


The Lost Referee

The referee was a student at a university called Birzeit, he is studying computer engineering and is in his fifth and final year. He is a social type, always volunteering at events and occasions, one of these volunteer work, was to Referee the football matches that were held every Tuesday and Thursday.

At the end of such day, when all the matches were done and referees job was done he set off to the changing rooms to wash up. As he reached the changing rooms he pushed the door and the rush of wind from the door brought him the taste of sulphur and smell of ash.

Entering the room the Referee caught the sound of humming in the back of the room, it seemed to be coming from behind one the changing curtains, he called out but there was no answer, so he decided to walk up to the curtain and open it. Behind it he found a large green sphere laying on the floor humming gently, the referee reached down and touched it, his out stretched fingers touched the surface and with a jolt of pain and everything went dark.

The referee woke up, got up and looked around, he was not in the changing room, he was in side street of a city, the city was unfamiliar and strange, a man in strange clothes walked by, the Referee ran after the man and caught up to him and asked,” Where am i?, what is this Place?”, and the man looked at him strange and replied, “Pizzora of course!, the greatest city in the lands.”.
